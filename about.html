Our Story

Where Psychology Meets Design

Founded in 2024, PERSONA emerged from a simple observation: everyone expresses their personality differently, yet most phone cases are generic and impersonal. We set out to change that.

Our journey began with extensive research into the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) and Swiss design principles. We discovered that the precision, minimalism, and systematic approach of Swiss typography perfectly complemented the structured nature of personality typing.
